FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ is 50 sets per color for car seat covers and 50 pieces per style for rugs and cushions. For medical products, MOQ starts at 50 pieces per size. We're flexible and can discuss smaller trial orders for new partners.

Can I get samples before placing a bulk order?

Yes! We offer free samples for qualified distributors and retailers. You only need to cover the shipping cost. Sample delivery typically takes 5-7 business days via express courier.

What is your production lead time?

Standard production time is 15-20 days after order confirmation and deposit. For large orders or custom designs, lead time may be 25-30 days. We'll provide an exact timeline when you request a quote.

Do you offer OEM/ODM services?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom manufacturing. We can produce products with your branding, custom packaging, specific colors, sizes, and designs. Our design team can also help develop new products based on your requirements.

What payment terms do you accept?

We accept T/T (Telegraphic Transfer) with 30% deposit and 70% balance before shipment. For established partners, we can discuss L/C or other payment arrangements. We also accept secure payments through Alibaba Trade Assurance.

What certifications do your products have?

Our manufacturing facility is ISO 9001 certified for quality management. Our medical products meet ISO 13485 standards. All products undergo SGS testing. We use eco-friendly, chrome-free tanning processes.
