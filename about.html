About Us

Our Story

Company Profile

JINAN CARSTAUTO ACCESSORIES CO.,LIMITED

We are a leading manufacturer and exporter of premium sheepskin products based in Jinan, China. With over 15 years of experience in the industry, we specialize in crafting high-quality sheepskin car seat covers, home accessories, and lifestyle products that combine luxury, comfort, and durability.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility spans 50,000 square meters and employs skilled artisans who understand the art of working with genuine Australian and New Zealand sheepskin. We source only the finest raw materials and maintain strict quality control throughout the production process.

Our Mission

To bring the natural comfort and elegance of premium sheepskin to customers worldwide, while maintaining ethical sourcing and sustainable practices.

Our Production Process

01

Raw Material Selection

We source only the finest sheepskins from Australia's premium sheep stations. Each hide undergoes strict quality grading.

Eco-Friendly Tanning
